# Filth Gauge hitting 100% triggers Goose Boss Mode, not a lose condition

When the Filth Gauge reaches 100%, The Goose transforms into Goose Boss Mode (Phase 2) instead of losing the game. This is the intended path to victory — not a punishment.

Most games treat a filling "bad" meter as a lose condition (e.g. a poison gauge, a corruption bar). We deliberately inverted this: 100% filth is the climax that empowers the player. The Goose becomes their true boss self precisely when the room is at its dirtiest.

**Considered alternatives:**
- **Time-based unlock** — Phase 2 unlocks after N seconds. Rejected: removes the connection between room state and player power. The transformation should feel earned by the bacteria's success, not by waiting.
- **Kill count unlock** — Phase 2 unlocks after defeating N Bacteria tiers. Rejected: decouples the power fantasy from the filth system, which is the core loop.
- **100% filth = lose** — the obvious reading of a filling bad meter. Rejected: kills the narrative arc. The Goose is a boss character — they don't lose to bacteria, they transform.
